Rules

Welcome to the guessing number game! The rules are to guess the correct number within 1 to 20! Your score starts with 20 points and with each failed attempt at guessing the correct number your score will have 1 point deducted! Incorreclty guess the number 20 times and when your score reaches 0 you will lose the game and the screen will turn red. If you correctly guess the score within 20 guesses you will win the game and the screen will turn green! If you want to play again you will either click the 'Again' button to play again and keep your highscore if you click the 'Reset' button your highscore will be reset to 0! Good luck!
